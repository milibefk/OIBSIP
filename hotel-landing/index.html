Haile Hotels and Resorts has given a great definition of hospitality by first commencing its service in the city of Hawassa, in 2010, with a 4-star standard further expanding its branches to 7 destinations across Ethiopia. Now the hospitality chain leads 8 destinations in total Hawassa (the pioneer destination), Arbaminch, Adama, Gondar, Batu-Ziway, Shashemene (currently not in function), Yaya-Athletics Village (Sululta near Addis Ababa) and the youngest and biggest 5-star project yet Haile Grand Addis Ababa. Our organizational culture is led by mantra “It is Possible”, or in Amharic, “ይቻላል”, a famous relatable phrase of the founder to signify our class and hardworking environment all channeling to exceeding our esteemed customers expectations.

Haile Resorts, with locations in Hawassa, Arba Minch, Gondar, and more,
offer a wide range of services and amenities for both leisure and business travelers. Guests can enjoy well-furnished rooms with scenic views, diverse dining options featuring local and international cuisines, and extensive recreational facilities, including pools, gyms, tennis courts, and playgrounds. The resorts also provide spa and wellness services, conference and wedding venues, and family-friendly entertainment options. Additional amenities such as free Wi-Fi, shuttle services, laundry, and concierge services enhance the overall guest experience across their locations.
